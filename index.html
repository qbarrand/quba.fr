Work

 CV (PDF)

CERN

Geneva, Switzerland
since September 2016
IT-CS-CE

I am currently working with the Communication Engineering team on network-related topics. My main occupations include:

  • software development (Python mostly, also some Perl)
  • automation and management of network services (Linux with Puppet)
  • 4th line support and troubleshooting
  • radio engineering for Wi-Fi
  • equipment benchmarking

SNCF Réseau

Paris, France
2013–2016
I&P LE TBS

I held a Systems & Networks Engineer position at SNCF Réseau where I implemented web technologies on various real-time systems onboard IRIS 320, a special high-speed train dedicated to railway measurement. I used the following technologies:

  • C# and Microsoft .NET
  • MongoDB
  • JavaScript (Node.js and AngularJS)
  • Linux KVM on Proxmox VE

Education

 CV (PDF)

ENSIIE Paris
TELECOM SudParis

Paris, France
Class of 2016

I did my first two years of engineering studies at ENSIIE Paris. During my last semester, from September 2015 to January 2016, I took a specialized track at TELECOM SudParis focusing on mobile cellular networks (LTE / 5G) and software-defined networking (SDN / NFV).

IUT Belfort-Montbéliard

Belfort, France
Class of 2013

I obtained a DUT Informatique in 2013, which is the French equivalent to a BSc in Computer Science. My main academic project consisted in the design and development of a web services platform for l'Usine à Belfort, a local coworking space.
During this cursus, I also co-founded HackGyver, Belfort's hackerspace.
